Shopping Media

Shopping media, also known as social commerce or shopping through social media platforms, has become an increasingly significant trend in the retail industry. This phenomenon integrates social media features with e-commerce functionalities, allowing users to browse, review, and purchase products directly within their favorite social media apps. Platforms like Instagram, Facebook, Pinterest, and TikTok have developed shopping features that enable businesses to set up virtual storefronts and link their products in posts, stories, and live videos.
